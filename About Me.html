Hi, I'm Yi.

In 2010 I came to the United State with mixed feelings of excitement and uncertainty about my career path.

Through extensive hands-on practice on graduate school projects at San Jose State University, and my 8 months internship at Lam Research, I joined a B2B startup Cloudwiz as the first designer on the team in 2016. I wore many hats using my visual, interaction, and research skills to create an Application Performance Platform from scratch. For three years working on the team, I also helped Cloudwiz attain a product-market fit and scale its offerings.

My 3 E design philosophies thatwhich I constantly applied to my design are Empathy, Engagement, and Empowerment. I want to empathize with users, and design an engageable product that users like to interact with, ultimately empowering users to achieve their goals.

Since 2020, I I'm currently preparing to relaunch my UX designer career by working as a freelance UX content producer focusing on enterprise product reviews, and level up my design skills by learning at Coursera. I'm always interested in a new challenge. Reach out to me : annefeng68+job@gmail.com
